Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2025

Welcome to Safa Demirkan's portfolio website. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you visit this site.

Information I Collect

I use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect pseudonymous data about your interactions with this website, including:

  • Page views, sessions, and time spent on the site.
  • Traffic sources (e.g., how you arrived at the site).
  • Device and browser information (e.g., browser type, operating system).
  • Geographic location (approximated via IP address).
  • Events such as form submissions and social link clicks.

I do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless you provide it voluntarily (e.g., via the Contact or Feedback forms).

How I Use Your Information

The data collected via Google Analytics is used to:

  • Understand visitor behavior to improve the website.
  • Track engagement with forms and social links.
  • Analyze traffic sources to optimize marketing efforts.

Cookies and Tracking

This site uses cookies via Google Analytics to track visitor activity. You will be prompted to consent to cookies when you first visit the site. You can withdraw consent by clearing your browser cookies and local storage.

Data Sharing

I do not share your data with third parties except as required by law. Google Analytics processes data on my behalf, and I have enabled IP anonymization to protect your privacy.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws, including the right to access, delete, or opt out of data collection. To exercise these rights, please contact me via the Contact form.

Contact Me

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the Contact form on the website.
